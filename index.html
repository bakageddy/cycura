About Cycura Minerals

A vertically integrated mineral recovery company specializing in sustainable battery material recycling.

Cycura Minerals is a vertically integrated mineral recovery company specializing in the hydrometallurgical extraction of lithium, nickel, cobalt, and manganese from end-of-life lithium-ion batteries.

We deliver battery-grade cathode materials through a proprietary low-emission, high-yield process optimized for NMC-based black mass.

Our innovative approach ensures over 92% recovery efficiency while maintaining the highest standards of purity and sustainability.

Our Recycling Process

Proprietary technology for extracting high-value minerals from lithium-ion batteries.

1

Collection & Sorting

End-of-life lithium-ion batteries are collected, safely discharged, and sorted by chemistry type.

2

Mechanical Processing

Mechanical shredding and separation to produce black mass containing valuable cathode materials.

3

Hydrometallurgical Extraction

Our proprietary process dissolves and selectively extracts lithium, nickel, cobalt, and manganese.

4

Purification & Crystallization

Advanced purification techniques to achieve 99.5%+ purity for battery-grade materials.
